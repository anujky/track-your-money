# Track Your Money - Quick Start Guide

## ğŸš€ Get Started in 2 Minutes

### Step 1: Open the App
1. Navigate to the project folder
2. Double-click `index.html` to open in your browser
3. Or use a local server:
   ```powershell
   python -m http.server 8000
   # Visit http://localhost:8000
   ```

### Step 2: Add Your First Transaction
1. Click **"Add Transaction"** in the navigation
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Date**: Auto-filled with today's date
   - **Type**: Select "Income", "Expense", "Saving", or "Investment"
   - **Category**: Select from predefined categories
   - **Sub-Category**: Choose a specific subcategory
   - **Description**: Optional
   - **Amount**: Enter the amount in rupees
3. Click **"Add Transaction"**

### Step 3: View Your Dashboard
1. Click **"Dashboard"** to see:
   - Summary cards with totals
   - Pie charts showing breakdown by category
   - Circular progress indicators showing percentages
   - Recent transactions list

### Step 4: Track a Loan (Optional)
1. Click **"Loans"**
2. Fill in loan details:
   - Loan name, principal amount
   - Interest rate and tenure (months)
   - EMI will auto-calculate
3. Click **"Add Loan"**
4. To record EMI payment:
   - Click **"Record EMI"** button
   - Enter payment date and amount
   - The due amount will update automatically

### Step 5: View Reports
1. Click **"Reports"**
2. Choose report type:
   - Monthly breakdown
   - Yearly summary
   - Category-wise analysis
3. Export to CSV for backup
